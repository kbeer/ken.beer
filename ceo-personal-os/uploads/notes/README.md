# Notes and Reference Material

## Purpose

This folder is for storing any notes, documents, or reference material that might inform your reviews and reflections.

## What to Upload

Drop any of the following here:
- Personal journals or weekly notes
- Strategy documents or business plans
- Meeting notes from key conversations
- Feedback from coaches, advisors, or team members
- Book notes or learning summaries
- Any other material that helps you reflect on your year

Accepted formats: `.md`, `.txt`, `.pdf`, `.docx`

## How to Use This

1. Upload relevant notes and documents
2. Reference them when completing your reviews
3. Extract key insights and add them to `memory.md`
4. Use them to inform goal-setting and decision-making

## Organization (Optional)

You can organize this folder however you like:
- By year (e.g., `2024/`, `2025/`)
- By topic (e.g., `strategy/`, `personal/`, `team/`)
